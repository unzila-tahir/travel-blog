Welcome To My Travel Blog

Hi! I m Unzila Tahir

I've always been an ardent reader of novels, and my fascination with Turkey has been fueled by the pages I've turned. The vivid descriptions of its historical wonders, bustling bazaars, and breathtaking landscapes have sparked a longing to explore this enchanting land in person. Turkey, for me, is not just a destination; it's a living chapter waiting to be experienced. The historical marvels of Turkey, as I've read in countless novels, offer a captivating journey through time. Istanbul, formerly Constantinople, with its Hagia Sophia and the iconic Blue Mosque, invites me to step into the same spaces that characters in my books might have explored. Then there's Ephesus, an ancient city where the ruins seem to whisper tales of their own, much like the pages of my favorite novels. But it's not just the history that beckons me; it's the landscapes that echo the settings of my most cherished stories. The Mediterranean and Aegean coasts promise idyllic scenes that mirror the pages of my reading adventures, with their beautiful beaches and clear waters. Cappadocia's surreal rock formations evoke the fantastical realms I've encountered in literature. Pamukkale, with its terraces of white mineral-rich water, feels like a page turned into reality. What truly ignites my interest is the cuisine and the people of Turkey. The aromatic spices, savory kebabs, and sweet baklava are akin to the culinary journeys I've taken through the narratives I've loved. And the warmth and hospitality of the Turkish people remind me of the characters who've left a mark on my heart. As I imagine myself exploring bustling bazaars, savoring the country's natural beauty, and perhaps even embarking on some adventure activities, I can't help but feel that Turkey is the place where novels come to life. It's the merging of my literary interests with the tangible experiences of real-world travel. Turkey, in my mind, is more than a place on the map; it's a vibrant narrative waiting for me to become a part of it.
